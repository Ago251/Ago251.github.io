About Me

From the beginning of my education, I pursued the goal of becoming a game programmer, choosing focused paths such as a technical institute in computer science and a specialized school in game programming.

Over time, I realized that I wanted to specialize in AI game programming, which led me to take a specialized course with a senior AI game programmer. This experience allowed me to deepen my understanding of the role and connect with industry professionals, including the AI game programmer for Little Nightmares 2.

However, before this course I had already gained experience in AI game programming through various projects, including a turn-based game developed during my studies where I created the enemy AI, and another project that is very close to my heart called Lost in Space, a survival horror game where I designed the AI controlling the zombies.

I would like to conclude with my life philosophy, which is "Learn, Live, and Enjoy." This approach underscores my belief in the continuous pursuit of knowledge, active engagement with life experiences, and integrating enjoyment into everything I do. It is this philosophy that fuels my determination to excel in the dynamic and ever-evolving field of game development.
